Car Rental Website Redesign

Design

Web-framing, Prototyping, Ideation

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Axure

Development

-

Redesign work of a car rental website. The aim is to improve its user experience, which enable users to make comparison between different car options. Transaction should also be performed easily by the users.

Front page is redesigned to into a more clean, simple and modernized fashion. Cards in flat design is used to display commercials, offers etc.

At least two cars are selected, users can check the techinical details in a comparison table.

Renter's information is provided in order to accompolish the transaction.

Collaborative Booth (Storyboard)

Design

Storyboard, Ideation, Concept Evaluation

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Development

-

The collaborative booth is a semi-opened area in an office environment, where people can collaborate with each other with a mirrored drawing surface and real size visual feed of remote people. It aims to provide remote meeting participants with the convenience of drawing on papers or whiteboards in the same room and closer intimacy of others.

Advanced setup with mirrored surface and real-size visual feed

This solution features with two screens, one vertical with video feed in real size. Below it, collaborative screen with mirrored canvas. Video capture whole upper body downwards to canvas, however canvas is outside video feed. This in order not to create ambiguous behaviour and desire to look at remote canvas inside of own. Collaboration takes place in booths with dedicated hardware. Booth is large enough for several persons.

Convinient setup with tablet and visual feed in lap-top screen size

This type of collaboration could also be performed using own laptop together with tablet. This would give advantage of being able to sit anywhere, but would give smaller canvas, smaller video feed without real size remote persons, and less convenience of setting up the system. This approach enables the system to be hardware independent.

UI design that rises Environmental awareness

Design

Illustration, Mobile UI-framing, Information Visualization

Tools

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Development

-

Exceeding shopping nowadays creates a lot of waste and extra cost in both materials and logistics. Mobile apps from big name brands are usually designed towards solely increasing the sales without letting people knows the knowledge behind. For example, if the workers were fairly paid during the production of a good. This UI design for a moile shopping app aims to better inform people with knowledge behind the curtain such as transportation cost, energy cunsumption, and if the workers were fairly paid etc.

Display of how menu button looks like on a mobile device.

Display of how search bar. The searching result is based on the current location of the user.

When dragging an item, a sidebar appears which provides actions that user can perform with the current dragged item.

Display of item description when its onclick.

Display of comparison view of environmental data, which indicates several environmental statistics regarding two selected items and how they are differentiated.

Display of comparison view of production regions.

Display of comparison view of trade value of two selected items.

Characters of a fiction

Design

Character Design, Sketching

Tools

Adobe Photoshop

Development

-

First step: I often start with defining character's faces. It is good to design it based on background story and character's strongest personality.

Second step: Narrow it down to a few of them, try to create facial expressions.

Third step: define the details of character's face. eyes, mouth, forehead space etc.

Forth step: define the character basic body measurements: height, leg length etc.

Fifth step: give the character a body shape! it is related to the muscle percentage etc.

Sixth step: create a draft of the character. Mostly sketching, with clothes, accessories, hair color etc.

Seventh step: make it colored!

Eighth step: create a story, moodboard behind the character. its background, family, friends related characters etc.
